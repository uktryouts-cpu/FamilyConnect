## How to Use the App - Simple Steps

### Step 1: Add Family Members
1. Click **"Add Family Member"** button
2. Enter their name, location, and any other details you know
3. Repeat until you've added several family members

üí° **Tip:** The more family members you add, the better matches we can find for you.

### Step 2: View Potential Matches
1. Go to the **"Leads"** section (or tap the link on the Dashboard)
2. You'll see a list of people who might be your relatives
3. Each person shows:
   - **Photo** - A thumbnail of their picture
   - **Name & Location** - Where they might be from
   - **Match Score** - How confident we are (0-100%)
   - **Why They Match** - The reason we think you're related

### Step 3: Connect with Matches
For each potential match, you have two buttons:
- **"Add to Tree"** - Add them directly to your family tree
- **"Contact"** - Send them a message asking to connect

### Step 4: Send a Connection Message
When you click "Contact":
1. Choose a message template or write your own
2. Pick from three friendly templates:
   - "Heritage Discovery" - Tell them you found genealogical connections
   - "Family Research" - Explain you're researching family history
   - "Reconnection" - Express interest in reconnecting
3. Customize the message if you like
4. Click "Send Message"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How does the app find matches?**  
A: It analyzes the family members you add and searches databases for people with similar names, locations, and family relationships.

**Q: Is my information really private?**  
A: Yes! Your data is encrypted and stays on your device. We never sell or share your information.

**Q: What if I don't get any matches?**  
A: Try adding more family members with as much detail as possible (full names, locations, dates). More information = better matches.

**Q: Can I remove someone from my family tree?**  
A: Yes! You can edit or delete any family member anytime.

**Q: What if I message someone and they don't respond?**  
A: That's normal. Not everyone actively uses genealogy apps. You can try again later or contact someone else.

## Troubleshooting

### No matches appearing?
- **Solution:** Add more family members with complete information
- Include names, locations, and approximate dates
- The more people you add, the more likely we'll find matches

### App running slowly?
- **Solution:** Close other apps and refresh the page
- If on mobile, check your internet connection

### Can't send a message to a match?
- **Solution:** Make sure you've entered a message
- Check that your internet connection is working
