## Match Confidence Scoring

Each potential match gets a score showing how likely the relationship is:

| Score | Meaning | Reliability |
|-------|---------|------------|
| 90-100% | Very likely family | High confidence |
| 70-89% | Likely family | Good confidence |
| 50-69% | Possible family | Moderate confidence |
| 0-49% | Long shot | Low confidence |

**What affects the score:**
- Shared surnames
- Geographic proximity (historical)
- Timeline compatibility
- Shared records or sources
- DNA-like naming patterns
- Ancestral records matches

## Tips for Better Results

### Finding Relatives Online

1. **Use Full Names**
   - Instead of "John", search "John William Smith"
   - Include middle names or initials

2. **Add Multiple Family Members**
   - Sentinel works better with more people
   - Add siblings, parents, grandparents
   - Include wives/husbands (maiden names)

3. **Include Locations**
   - Add where ancestors lived
   - Include migration patterns
   - Note historical origins

4. **Search Different Variations**
   - Try name variations (Jon vs John)
   - Try different spelling variations
   - Try former addresses or cities

5. **Check Multiple Sources**
   - Use all four search sources
   - Some records only appear in specific databases
   - Combine results for better picture

### Connecting with People

1. **Verify First**
   - Check if they look like family
   - Review their information
   - Start with question in message

2. **Be Respectful**
   - Not everyone wants family connections
   - Explain why you're contacting them
   - Be patient for responses

3. **Share Gradually**
   - Don't overwhelm with too much info
   - Build trust gradually
   - Share documents and records over time

## Common Questions

### Q: How accurate is Sentinel AI?
**A:** The AI uses multiple factors to score matches. Scores above 70% are usually reliable. Always verify with records or direct contact.

### Q: Can I search before adding family members?
**A:** You can search the Advanced Search tab anytime, but Sentinel AI works best with at least 2-3 family members added.

### Q: What if I get a lot of false matches?
**A:** Look for matches with 75%+ confidence. Check the "Reason" field. You can also filter by location to narrow results.

### Q: How do app user notifications work?
**A:** When you add an app user to your tree or send a connection request, they get a notification. They can then accept or decline.

### Q: Can I save information about non-users?
**A:** Yes! You can add anyone you find online to your tree. You can save their information and links to online records.

### Q: Is my search private?
**A:** Yes. Searches are private. Only when you send a connection request does the other person know you found them.

### Q: Can I undo an add or connection request?
**A:** Yes. You can remove connections anytime from your family tree. Pending requests can be cancelled.

## Troubleshooting

### Sentinel isn't finding matches
- Add more family members
- Include middle names and birth years
- Add locations and migration history
- Activate Sentinel multiple times (learns over time)

### Search returns no results
- Try different name spellings
- Use shorter search terms
- Try different source
- Include location information

### Can't send connection request
- Make sure they're an app user
- Check that message isn't empty
- Verify email address is correct
- Try again in a few moments

### Person disappeared from matches
- Might have updated their profile
- AI might have updated confidence score
- You might have already connected with them
- They might have deleted their profile
